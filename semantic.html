Sports are cool!

All about sports

There are many variations of sports, but all with the same purpose! Sports can help you stay healthy not only physically but also mentally while having a good time!

Sports can be played or watched. Sometimes athletes are so good at playing a sport, that they are paid millions of dollars each years. Normally people watch these athletes play their sport from home or at the site of the game/match/meet. Those who just play for fun have a number of sports to choose from all that require different landscapes, amount of people and equipment.

There are some sports that I love to watch but could never play and some I love to play but hate to watch. For example I love watching ice hockey games, but there is no way I could ever play hockey. Another example is that I love to play golf but I find it so boring to watch
